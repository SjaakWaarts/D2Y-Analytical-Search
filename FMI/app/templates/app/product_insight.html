Uptake of new Ingredients

This dataset contains the results of the Peer Company patent and article review. Thomson Innovation is used for the patents and Chemical Abstracts for the articles.

Predict the future uptake of new ingredients. This is done by first finding similair ingredients and based on the historical usage of those similar ingredients, the usage or uptake of the new ingredient is predicted. Similar ingredients are found by correlating the characteristics of the new ingredient with the same characteristics of existing ingredients. The characteristics used for this correlation are: classification, used in formulas, sold in regions, cost, natural
